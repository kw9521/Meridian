About Meridian

Welcome to Meridian, your go-to platform for exploring stock market trends, tracking portfolios, and learning about investing. Our goal is to make investing in stocks more accessible, engaging, and fun for everyone, regardless of experience level!

Our Mission

At Meridian, we aim to provide you with a website to practice and learn investing with simulated funds. The insights you gain from your time with Meridian, will give you the skills to succeed in investigating with the real stock market. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced investor, our platform gives you the tools to make informed decisions.
